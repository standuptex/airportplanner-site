Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 2025

Airport Planner does not collect, sell, or share personal information. If you enable location on your device, it is used only on your device to estimate drive time. We do not track users across apps or websites.

Data We Use

  • Location (optional) — used solely to estimate drive time to the airport; not stored or shared.

User Privacy Choices

Airport Planner does not require an account or collect personal data. As such, there are no user privacy choices to manage. All calculations occur on-device, and you can disable location access at any time in your iOS Settings.
